Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) - Häufig gestellte Fragen

Englisch Version (see below for German)

How can I book a room?

You can book a room through our website by clicking on "Book" and entering your details.

Are there parking facilities at the hotel?

Yes, we offer paid parking facilities for our guests.

Where can I find the imprint page?

It is linked in the bottom of every top-level page on our site.

What is the check-in and check-out time?

Check-in is available from 3:00 PM, and check-out is until 12:00 PM noon.

Is there Wi-Fi available at the hotel?

Yes, we provide Wi-Fi for all our guests.

Are pets allowed at the hotel?

Unfortunately, we do not allow pets in the hotel.

Can I cancel my booking?

Yes, you can cancel your booking, but our cancellation policy applies. Please check it on our website.

Is there a restaurant at the hotel?

Yes, we have an on-site restaurant where you can enjoy breakfast, lunch, and dinner.

Do you offer non-smoking rooms at the hotel?

Yes, we have non-smoking rooms for guests who do not smoke.

Is there an airport shuttle service?

Yes, we provide an airport shuttle service. Please contact us for more information.

How can I specify additional requests or special preferences?

You can specify any additional requests or special preferences during the booking process in the "Special Requests" field.

Is there a fitness center at the hotel?

Yes, we have a fitness center available for our guests.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept credit cards and cash as payment methods.

Do you offer in-room breakfast service?

Yes, we offer room service for breakfast. Please check the menu options.

Is there a pool at the hotel?

Yes, we have an outdoor pool for our guests.

Does the hotel have accessible facilities?

Yes, our hotel is wheelchair accessible and provides accessible rooms and facilities.

Can I get a baby cot in the room?

Yes, we provide baby cots upon request free of charge.

Are there smoking rooms at the hotel?

No, our hotel is smoke-free. Smoking is only allowed in designated areas.

Does the hotel offer laundry service?

Yes, we provide laundry service for our guests. Details can be found in your room.

How far is the hotel from the city center?

Our hotel is approximately 2 kilometers from the city center.

Does the hotel have meeting rooms?

Yes, we have meeting rooms available for business meetings and events. Please contact us for reservations.
